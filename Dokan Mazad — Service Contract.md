# Marketing Services Agreement

---

**This agreement is entered into as of _______________**

**Between:**

**SIX% MARKETING** ("Agency"), represented by _________

**and**

**Dokan Mazad** ("Client"), represented by _________

Together referred to as "the Parties."

---

## Purpose

This agreement establishes a three-month trial period during which both Parties work together to define the brand's direction, identify and lock in on the target market, and build the foundation for long-term growth. The trial period is not a test of effort — it is the time required to do the work correctly. At the end of three months, both Parties review what has been built and agree on the path forward.

---

## 1. Scope of Services

The Agency agrees to provide the following services to the Client on a monthly basis:

- Video editing
- Post design
- Copywriting (scripts, captions, and series copy)
- Social media plan
- Social media management
- Marketing plan and funnel development
- Full team direction
- Consultation and refinement
- Engagement management

---

## 2. Monthly Deliverables and Targets

The Agency commits to delivering the following each month:

**Recurring monthly:**
- 15 edited videos
- 10 designed posts
- 1 monthly content calendar, delivered before the start of each month
- 1 monthly social media plan
- 1 monthly performance report, delivered no later than the 5th of the following month
- Ongoing engagement management — responses to comments and DMs within 24 hours during business days

**One-time deliverables, upon completion:**
- Full marketing funnel
- FAQ document: the Agency provides the Client with a list of the most common buyer questions. The Client is responsible for supplying the answers. The Agency formats, edits, and delivers the final document once the Client's answers are received.

One-time deliverables are produced during the Trial Period and are considered complete once approved by the Client in writing. They do not repeat monthly.

The termination clause in Section 7.1 — failure to deliver agreed services — is assessed against the deliverables listed in this section. The deliverable targets above apply from Month 2 onwards. Month 1 deliverables are agreed in writing by both Parties at signing and reflect the reduced production capacity of the Onboarding Period.

---

## 3. Onboarding Period

The first **ten (10) business days** of the engagement are designated as the Onboarding Period.

During this period, the Agency will present prototypes covering content types, design direction, and the agreed-upon points discussed prior to signing. The Client will review and approve these prototypes before full production begins.

Any adjustments to the agreed direction will be made during this period. Once prototypes are approved in writing, they serve as the foundation for all work that follows.

The Onboarding Period is included within the first paid month and does not extend the contract term.

---

## 4. Production Costs

Production costs — including but not limited to filming, professional equipment, and brand ambassador fees — are not included in the monthly retainer. These costs vary by content type and scale.

The Parties agree to one of the following production models, selected at signing:

**Option A — Per-Shoot Approval**
The Agency provides a cost estimate for each production. Work begins only upon written approval from the Client.

**Option B — Monthly Production Budget**
A fixed monthly production budget is agreed upon at the start of each month. The Agency operates within that budget.

**Selected model:** ☐ Option A / ☐ Option B

---

## 5. Monthly Fee

The Client agrees to pay the Agency a monthly retainer of **$1,800 USD**.

Payment is delivered in person at the start of each month before work begins. Both Parties sign acknowledgment of receipt at the time of payment.

If payment is not received within 10 business days of the due date, the Client must provide written cause for the delay via WhatsApp. Failure to provide written cause within that period constitutes grounds for immediate termination under Section 7.3.

---

## 6. Contract Term

This agreement begins on _______________ and runs for an initial term of **three (3) months** ("the Trial Period"), ending on _______________.

Following the Trial Period, the agreement continues on an extendable term as agreed upon by both Parties, with renegotiable deliverables and fees, unless terminated in accordance with Section 7.

---

## 7. Termination

### 7.1 Termination During the Trial Period

During the three-month Trial Period, the Client may exit the contract under the following conditions:

- The basis for exit must be failure by the Agency to deliver the agreed services as defined in Section 2.
- The Client must provide written notice of dissatisfaction to the Agency no later than **7 days before the end of the paid month**.
- Upon receipt of notice, the Parties will meet within 3 business days to discuss the issue.
- If the issue is not resolved to the Client's satisfaction within that discussion, the contract dissolves at the end of the paid month.
- No refund will be issued for the month in which notice is given.
- No further payment obligations exist beyond the current paid month.

The Agency may not be held in breach for outcomes outside its direct control, including but not limited to platform algorithm changes, filming delays caused by the Client, or failure to approve content within agreed timelines.

### 7.2 Termination After the Trial Period

After the Trial Period, either party may terminate the agreement at the end of any month by providing written notice at least **7 days before the end of that month**.

The final month of service will be completed and billed in full. No refund will be issued for a month already paid.

### 7.3 Immediate Termination

Either party may terminate the agreement immediately and without notice in the event of:

- A material breach of this agreement that is not remedied within 7 days of written notice
- Non-payment beyond 10 business days of the due date without written cause provided by the Client
- Actions by either party that are unlawful or damaging to the other party's reputation

---

## 8. Content Approval

The Agency will submit content for Client approval before publication. The Client agrees to provide feedback or approval within **3 business days** of submission.

If no response is received within 3 business days, the Agency may proceed with publication or hold the content at its discretion.

The Agency will revise submitted content until the desired outcome is reached and the Client is satisfied, provided that feedback is specific, actionable, and consistent with the approved brand guidelines.

---

## 9. Client Responsibilities

The Client is responsible for:

- Providing filming access, locations, and on-camera talent as required for production
- Supplying brand assets, account credentials, and any materials necessary for the Agency to perform its services
- Providing written approvals, feedback, or responses within 3 business days of any Agency request
- Supplying answers to the FAQ question list provided by the Agency within an agreed timeframe
- Ensuring all information provided to the Agency is accurate and up to date

Delays caused by the Client's failure to meet these responsibilities do not constitute a breach by the Agency and do not entitle the Client to a refund or contract extension.

---

## 10. Scope Changes

Any deliverable or service not listed in Sections 1 or 2 falls outside the scope of this agreement. Additional services require a separate written agreement and agreed fee before work begins.

Verbal requests do not constitute an agreed scope change. All scope changes must be confirmed in writing by both Parties.

---

## 11. Exclusivity

For the duration of this agreement, the Agency will not take on a direct competitor of Dokan Mazad operating in the Korean car import and export market targeting Arab buyers.

This exclusivity applies solely to direct competitors in the above-defined market and does not restrict the Agency from working with clients in other industries or sectors.

---

## 12. Intellectual Property

All content produced by the Agency under this agreement becomes the property of the Client upon receipt of full payment for the month in which it was produced.

Upon termination of the agreement and receipt of all outstanding payments, all accounts, content libraries, designs, and digital assets created under this contract are transferred in full to the Client.

Unpaid work remains the property of the Agency.

The Agency retains the right to use completed work in its portfolio unless the Client requests otherwise in writing.

---

## 13. Non-Solicitation

The Client agrees not to directly hire, contract, or engage any individual member of the Agency's team during the term of this agreement and for a period of **12 months** following its termination.

This clause applies regardless of whether the individual approaches the Client directly.

---

## 14. Confidentiality

Both Parties agree to keep confidential all business information, strategies, pricing, client data, and internal communications shared during this engagement.

This obligation remains in effect for **24 months** following termination of the agreement.

---

## 15. Force Majeure

Neither Party shall be considered in breach of this agreement if performance is prevented or delayed by circumstances genuinely beyond their reasonable control, including but not limited to acts of war, natural disasters, government restrictions, or platform shutdowns.

The affected Party must notify the other in writing within **5 business days** of the event. If the situation persists beyond **30 days**, either Party may terminate the agreement without penalty.

---

## 16. Liability

The Agency is not liable for:

- Results, sales, or revenue outcomes — marketing results depend on many variables outside the Agency's control
- Platform algorithm changes or policy updates
- Delays or quality issues caused by the Client's failure to provide materials, approvals, or direction on time
- Any third-party costs including but not limited to paid advertising spend, platform fees, or production costs approved under Section 4

The Agency's total liability under this agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid in the three months preceding the claim.

---

## 17. Communication

The Parties agree to maintain regular communication through **WhatsApp**.

The Agency will provide a monthly performance report no later than the 5th day of the following month.

Response time from the Agency during business hours: within **24 hours**.

---

This agreement is governed by the laws of the **Republic of Turkey**. Any disputes arising from this agreement shall be resolved in the courts of **Istanbul, Turkey**.

---

This document constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties and supersedes all prior discussions, proposals, or representations. Any changes to this agreement must be made in writing and signed by both Parties.
